# Vendor Disclosure Questions

Boundary: public buyer-readiness questions only. Do not request private implementation details, production keys, patent claim charts, or confidential customer architecture.

1. What categories of autonomous action can create external impact?
2. How do you distinguish tool access from action acceptance?
3. What evidence categories are checked before high-consequence action?
4. How do you handle stale approvals, revocation, replay, and duplicate action attempts?
5. What conditions cause HOLD rather than ACCEPT?
6. What conditions cause REFUSE?
7. How is human escalation documented?
8. What public evidence can a buyer review without receiving private mechanisms?
9. Is there a separate conformance or independent verification path?
10. How are incidents and near misses converted into readiness improvements?
