# Buyer Due-Diligence Checklist

- [ ] External-impact action categories are named.
- [ ] Protected systems are identified.
- [ ] Tool access is separated from action acceptance.
- [ ] Freshness and revocation are discussed.
- [ ] Replay or duplicate action handling is discussed.
- [ ] HOLD and REFUSE conditions are documented.
- [ ] Human escalation owner is named.
- [ ] Vendor response avoids unsupported approval claims.
- [ ] Separate conformance evidence is requested when readiness discussion is not enough.

Boundary: buyer readiness discussion only; not procurement approval.
