# RFP Clause Library

## Action-level evidence request

The vendor should describe, in public-safe terms, how autonomous actions that can create external impact are identified, paused, escalated, or refused before impact.

## HOLD conditions

The vendor should describe examples of incomplete, stale, ambiguous, revoked, duplicate, or out-of-scope evidence conditions that would lead to a HOLD discussion.

## Independent evidence handoff

Where public readiness discussion is insufficient, the vendor should identify whether separate conformance artifacts or independent verification evidence can be reviewed.

Boundary: clause library for buyer discussion only; not legal advice, compliance approval, or certification.
