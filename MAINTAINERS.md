# Maintainers

Initial stewardship: Meridian Verity Group.

This public readiness kit is intentionally stewarded as public category and education infrastructure, not as a product implementation, standard, certification kit, conformance program, or foundation-hosted project.

## Maintainer responsibilities

- Preserve the public/private boundary.
- Review scenario-card contributions for clarity, human impact, and rights-boundary language.
- Reject implementation-level disclosures, exact evidence schemas, cryptographic mechanisms, non-bypassable enforcement designs, patent claim charts, partner deployment details, and certification/conformance claims.
- Keep `make qa` passing before release tags.
- Keep README language aligned with: “free public readiness kit with a 5-minute educational scenario-card linter for developers.”

## External maintainers

Future external maintainers should be added only after they understand and agree to the public/private boundary, contribution licensing, and no-certification/no-standard/no-patent-license posture.
