# 2-Hour Incident Tabletop

Goal: convert a near miss or hypothetical incident into readiness questions.

Agenda:

1. Reconstruct the action, actor, target, protected system, and consequence.
2. Identify which evidence was missing, stale, ambiguous, or contradicted.
3. Decide whether the scenario should be ACCEPT, HOLD, or REFUSE in tabletop discussion.
4. Record escalation path and owner.
5. Update readiness index responses.

Boundary: tabletop learning only; not incident certification, compliance conclusion, legal advice, or production safety case.
