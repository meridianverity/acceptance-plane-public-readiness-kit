# 30-Minute Board Briefing

Goal: establish why autonomous action needs an acceptance boundary before impact.

Agenda:

1. Five minutes: access, permission, execution, and acceptance are different questions.
2. Ten minutes: review three scenario cards.
3. Ten minutes: identify the board-level readiness question.
4. Five minutes: decide whether a readiness index session is needed.

Boundary: public education only; not certification, compliance approval, production readiness, or an allow/deny decision.
