# Standards Engagement Guardrails

This public kit is not a standard. Standards engagement should be careful, staged, and rights-aware.

## Safe engagement

- Discuss vocabulary and problem framing.
- Share public scenario cards.
- Ask for action acceptance language in safety and procurement frameworks.
- Encourage recognition that access control, attestation, and logging are necessary but not sufficient.

## Avoid

- Publishing exact schemas.
- Publishing cryptographic binding details.
- Publishing conformance vectors.
- Granting implied certification rights.
- Allowing third parties to claim Acceptance Plane certification without authorization.

## Positioning

The public goal is a shared question: should this exact action be accepted now? The production mechanism remains a licensed technology path.
