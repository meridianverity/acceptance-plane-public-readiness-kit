# 12-Month Stewardship Roadmap

## Quarter 1

Publish the category, scenario corpus, and RFP kit. Establish language discipline.

## Quarter 2

Grow domain adoption through public-sector, healthcare, financial, cloud, education, sensor, robotics, and AR/VR communities.

## Quarter 3

Build public-interest coalitions around action acceptance before impact. Encourage buyers to request verifier-ready evidence and fail-closed posture.

## Quarter 4

Prepare licensed production tracks, partner pilots, and domain-specific implementations while preserving public/private boundaries.

## Permanent principle

Public literacy should grow faster than autonomous deployment risk, while production mechanisms remain responsibly controlled.
