# Production Boundary Readiness

This public file helps partners understand the boundary between public readiness materials and non-public production technology.

## Public materials may support

- Education.
- Procurement scoping.
- Scenario workshops.
- Domain framing.
- Readiness conversations.

## Non-public production materials may be required for

- Production enforcement.
- Cryptographic verification.
- Permit or receipt issuance.
- Non-bypassable gating.
- Conformance testing.
- Certification or registry participation.
- Hardware, OS, cloud, healthcare, sensor, AR/VR, robotics, or financial integrations.

## Partner readiness questions

- Which protected systems are in scope?
- Which high-consequence actions require acceptance control?
- Which domain regulations and governance constraints apply?
- What audit, insurance, procurement, or regulator evidence is required?
- Which production mechanism path is appropriate?
