# 90-Day Category Launch Plan

## Days 1–15: Foundation

- Publish the public readiness kit.
- Pin the canonical definition.
- Publish the public boundary statement.
- Release 10 scenario cards per week on social channels.
- Invite comments on vocabulary, not implementation details.

## Days 16–45: Adoption

- Run public webinars for cloud, healthcare, finance, education, and public-sector audiences.
- Share the RFP questionnaire with buyers and analysts.
- Invite universities and nonprofits to use the scenario corpus.
- Collect additional public scenarios.

## Days 46–75: Institutionalization

- Publish domain-specific readiness briefs.
- Create buyer-facing one-pagers.
- Encourage organizations to include “action acceptance before impact” in procurement language.
- Begin private partner conversations for licensed production mechanisms.

## Days 76–90: Stewardship

- Release v0.2 with refined scenarios and public feedback.
- Publish a transparency note describing what remains intentionally private.
- Prepare licensed mechanism pathways without merging them into the public kit.
