# Vendor Email Template

Subject: Action acceptance before impact — architecture questions

Hello [Vendor],

We are evaluating agentic AI systems that may affect protected systems, customer data, regulated records, production infrastructure, or workflow execution.

Before we proceed, please answer the following:

1. Which autonomous or semi-autonomous actions can your system initiate?
2. Where does AI intention become protected-system consequence?
3. What evidence is evaluated at that acceptance boundary?
4. How do you distinguish identity, access, delegation, runtime attestation, and action acceptance?
5. What causes an action to be accepted, held, or refused?
6. What happens when evidence is stale, revoked, ambiguous, or unavailable?
7. What structured record can show why a specific autonomous action was accepted before impact?

We are not asking for proprietary implementation details in the first response. We are asking for clear architecture-level readiness.
