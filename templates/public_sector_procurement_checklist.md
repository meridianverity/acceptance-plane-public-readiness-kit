# Public Sector Procurement Checklist

Public agencies should require vendors to explain action acceptance before impact for AI systems that affect citizens, public benefits, permitting, education, healthcare, justice, infrastructure, or emergency services.

## Minimum public questions

- What actions can change a citizen-facing record, benefit, status, priority, communication, or physical condition?
- Which actions require human review?
- Which actions fail closed if evidence is missing or stale?
- Can the agency verify why an action was accepted?
- Can affected people challenge or understand the basis of an accepted action?
- Are logs post-hoc only, or is there pre-impact acceptance evidence?
- Does the vendor claim certification? If yes, who authorized it and how is it independently verified?
