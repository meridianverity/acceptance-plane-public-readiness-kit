# Nonprofit and Civil-Society Adoption Guide

Civil-society organizations can use the Acceptance Plane vocabulary to ask institutions and vendors better questions about AI systems that affect people.

## Questions to ask

- What real-world actions can the AI system cause?
- Who can be harmed if the wrong action is accepted?
- What evidence is required before the action happens?
- Can the affected person or institution understand why it was accepted?
- What happens when evidence is stale, missing, or contradictory?
- Does the system pause or refuse high-risk actions, or merely log them later?

## Public-interest use

Use scenario cards for workshops, public comments, procurement advocacy, safety reviews, and education.
