# Board Briefing: Action Acceptance Before Impact

## The issue

AI agents are moving from producing outputs to taking actions. A helpful plan, valid identity, or protected runtime does not by itself prove that a specific action should affect a protected system right now.

## The question

Can we prove why an autonomous action was accepted before it became real?

## What leadership should require

- Inventory high-consequence autonomous actions.
- Identify acceptance boundaries.
- Require gate-time evidence, not only logs after impact.
- Define ACCEPT / HOLD / REFUSE behavior.
- Fail closed for high-consequence actions when proof is missing or fails.
- Preserve verifier-ready evidence for audit, insurance, regulator, and customer trust.
