Why it matters

Not just a report. Not just a replay.

A public conformance corpus lets reviewers reproduce reference receipts, inspect EvidencePacks, validate fail-closed cases, and forward a bounded evidence artifact into architecture-fit or diligence review.

  • ACCEPT vectors show fresh, scoped, unreplayed proof.
  • HOLD vectors show remediable evidence gaps.
  • FAIL vectors show replay, mismatch, scope, temporal, and policy failures.
  • Every reference receipt is digest-bound and non-authorizing.
